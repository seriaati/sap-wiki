# Product

## Register

product

## Users

SAP players at two depths: casual players doing quick mid-session lookups ("what does Ant do at level 2?"), and competitive/theorycrafters doing deep cross-pet comparison and ability planning. Both arrive with a specific question; neither wants friction.

## Product Purpose

A fast, clean reference wiki for Super Auto Pets. Covers pets and foods. Data sourced from game dump — authoritative, not community-edited. Success: player finds what they need in under 5 seconds and trusts the data.

## Brand Personality

Playful, precise, trustworthy. SAP's own visual language is cute cartoon animals with soft colors and readable stats — the wiki should feel like it belongs in that world, not like a corporate database dropped on top of it.

## Anti-references

- Fandom/Wikia: ad-cluttered, visually noisy, slow, hostile layout
- Generic "dark dashboard" wikis: clinical and cold, wrong register for a cute auto-battler
- Bloated sidebars with navigation trees, recent-changes widgets, community portals

## Design Principles

1. **Playful precision** — inherit SAP's cartoon warmth; never sacrifice data clarity for decoration
2. **Zero-friction lookup** — the answer is the product; chrome, nav, and layout serve the answer, not themselves
3. **Comparison-ready layout** — competitive players scan multiple entries; card structure and stat alignment must support side-by-side reading
4. **Art-forward** — game sprites and icons are first-class content, not decoration; they carry the SAP identity
5. **Earned whitespace** — breathe without sprawling; dense enough for theorycrafters, clear enough for casual players

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Icons must have text labels (game sprites alone are not sufficient). Stat values need sufficient contrast against backgrounds.
